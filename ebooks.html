e-books

For my design fellowship, I converted many print publications into e-books, translating original print design files into EPUB (iPad, Nook, Kobo) and MOBI (Kindle) files. It was a challenge translating beautiful designs that were meant for print into a digital re-flowable format, but facing these challenges forced me to think of new solutions for converting print design files into e-books. It taught me how digital reflow works differently, and I had to make decisions about changing the original design, order, and even content to fit the context of a digital device.

I had the great opportunity to work on many Art of Disney books such as Art of Tangled, Art of Inside Out, and Art of Pixar. Below are some of my favorite e-books I converted.

Trek

This is personal project I developed based an idea from a design-thinking workshop, where my workshop partner told me about his desire for more independent, laid-back traveling without joining a tour group. To address this problem, I created Trek, a mobile app that reinvents the traveling experience by bringing individuals together when they seek an adventure.

Team

Individual Project

Role

UX, UI, visual design, branding

Process

User flow, wireframe, visual design, usability

My friends' Frustrations of Traveling

Relying on a tour group leads to less flexibility and independence
Fear of being lonely when you travel by yourself
Desire to travel independently without having to make detailed plans
Desire for a more authentic, less tourist-like experience

Wireframe

Solution

Trek makes it easier for travelers to connect with people and places by allowing users to attend small location-based meetups that any user can create. Trek eliminates the frustrations of traveling by providing company, flexibility, and ideas. As treks can be created by locals that wish to show travelers around, it also makes it easier for travelers to find adventures that may be less famous to tourists.

Users can easily browse, save, and attend treks, which are limited to 6 people to create an intimate and easy experience.

Upcoming, saved, and past treks are stored in your profile. Connect with other people from your trek by using the chat.

Type in any search term or search treks by category.

Anyone can create a trek.

Further Development

The usage of "tour guides" rather than a regular host, where users could create small tours and get paid

A rating system for hosts/guides/travelers

A more sophisticated system of features for different types of users (a tour guide, regular host, or traveler)

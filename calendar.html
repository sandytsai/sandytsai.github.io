Astrological Calendar

chronicle books design fellowship

The creative director asked us to create a calendar concept. I designed this astrological perpetual calendar that can be used for any year. I presented the prototype to the creative director, industrial designer, and editors.

Intimate Astronomy

I wanted to create a calendar that reflected the theme of time and space while also being more interactive. I came up with the idea of an astrological perpetual calendar that can sit intimately on a user's desk. The calendar's value lies mostly in its purpose as a display piece that a user can lightly interact with, showing the date on a beautiful calendar.

The separate parts were created using a cutting plotter, then put together using a mechanical screw. I designed and produced this prototype from concept to execution, and the industrial designer taught me how to implement the design with a plotting cutter.

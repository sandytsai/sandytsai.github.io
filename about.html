designer and cat stalker
I am a print and digital designer with a background in graphic design and web development. I have experience in visual and product design, and I have worked in publishing, tech, and branding in Los Angeles, San Francisco, and Taipei.

I aim to design experiences that engage the audience or user somehow--emotionally, visually, and intellectually. I love designs that are full of whimsy and personality, and similarly make an effort to incorporate that to my work if appropriate. I care about UI/UX, branding, hand-lettering, craft, books, technology, and cats.

about sandytsai.co

I designed and coded this website from the ground up using Atom and ported the website to Github. I created and photographed all the artwork that is within the project scope, including the hand-lettering on this page, unless stated otherwise. This site is still a work-in-progress; more to come: Instagram feed and potentially a blog section.

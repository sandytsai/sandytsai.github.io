designer and cat stalker

I am a print and digital designer with a background in graphic design and web development. I have experience in visual and product design, and I have worked in publishing, tech, and branding in Los Angeles, San Francisco, and Taipei.

I aim to design experiences that engage the audience or user somehow--emotionally, visually, and intellectually. I love designs that are full of whimsy and personality, and similarly make an effort to incorporate that to my work if appropriate. I care about UI/UX, branding, hand-lettering, craft, books, and technology.

I designed and coded this website, which is hosted on Github. I created and photographed all the artwork that is within the project scope, including the hand-lettering, unless stated otherwise.

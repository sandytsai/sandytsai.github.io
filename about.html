I am a UX & visual designer

I strongly believe in using UX for good, which is why I aim to understand humans deeply to improve lives. I draw on my art and design background to empathize with how people experience products and create experiences that not only add efficiency and value, but are delightful to use.

Previously, I worked as a graphic designer at the intersection of visual design and technology, merging my creative and technical side by transforming print into digitally interactive mediums. Working between print and digital exposed me to the limitations of static design tools in an increasingly dynamic world, motivating me to become a UX designer.

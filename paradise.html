Paradise is Near

At Chronicle Books, I was in charge of designing boarding pass invitations for a collaborative project between the design fellows. We planned an island themed party and came up with a consistent visual identity to design different materials. You can read more about the event here on the Chronicle Books Blog.

Client

Chronicle Books

Process

1 month for research, ideation, design, mockups

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop, cutting tools

Cohesive System

The other fellows also created teaser posters, postcards, signage, menus, and an island logo and flag for the event.

Images for the boarding passes were taken from the New York Public Library Digital Collection.

Paradise is Near

I was in charge of designing boarding pass invitations for a collaborative project between the design fellows. We planned an internal island themed party to celebrate the fellowship and designed promotional and decorative materials for the event. We collaboratively came up with a consistent visual identity and each designed different materials.

The other fellows also created teaser posters, postcards, signage, menus, and an island logo and flag for the event. You can read more about the event here on the Chronicle Books Blog.

Images for the boarding passes were taken from the New York Public Library Digital Collection.

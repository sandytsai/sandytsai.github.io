Rightest Care

As a UI designer for Vetica, I had the opportunity to redesign Rightest CARE, a diabetes management app designed and developed by Bionime Corporation. Bionime is a biotechnology and medical testing company that designs, develops, and sells blood glucose devices and management systems.

Process

Client

Bionime Corporation

Team

Kimi Huang (Art Director)

Process

2 weeks for wireframe and visual design

The Challenge

My task was to redesign the UI in a visual language that reflects Bionime’s rebrand. I also faced the challenge of improving the usability of the app while meeting the constraints of the client, which is to keep the original app’s overall structure and user flow. We also did not have resources or time to perform user research or interviews, only secondary research.

Target Users

“I need to how my diet affects my blood glucose levels so I can better manage my diabetes.” “I want to check in with my patients easily and be accessible when they need me.

The Metafesto

personal bookbinding project

This is a personal book project that I designed and hand-bound together. It is a collection of my personal hand-lettered typography work. The title is hand-lettered and a play on the words "meta" and "manifesto."
I wanted to experiment with material and textures, so the cover interior was made using many layers of gesso and covered with glossy black ink. I liked the imperfections from the ink, and I also left the binding thread out to make the hand made construction visible.
I painted the inside cover with black ink, and was happy with the imperfect drip textures that resulted.
The first spread is my personal design and life manifesto, and the second spread is a collection of sketches, doodles, and illustrations from work I created.
Even though the cover has more of a classic sophisticated style, I wanted my "designs mission" spread to be playful to contrast with the black elegant exterior.

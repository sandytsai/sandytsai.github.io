Airbnb Design Internship

This past summer I was an Experience Design intern on the Host Growth Activation team at Airbnb. I worked on a team with designers, engineers, data scientists, researchers, and PMs to grow our home host supply. Our team owns the List Your Space flow - the feature that hosts use to create new property listings on Airbnb. Our core objective is to increase new active listings and first bookings through List Your Space. I had the opportunity to own a variety of projects where I designed and prototyped new experiences for List Your Space on web and native. Since these projects have not been launched, please get in touch if you would like to know more.
